Creating strategically planned infrastructure

Making electromobility more accessible, efficient and sustainable.

Problem

End consumer

Missing infrastructure, limited wall boxes per street, poor infrastructure negatively impacts EV purchase decisions, slow progress in sustainable mobility.

Charging point operator

Unreliable foundation in location planning, lack of databases and insights, suboptimal choices, overlooking high-potential sites.

Our Approach

Data-based location assessment

Connecting financial and spatial analysis with key factors for high-traffic locations.

Evaluation and optimization

Determining high-potential sites, optimization of active charging points using data.

Data Basis

Charging stations with known consumption, POIs weighted by type and distance, regression model for predicted energy usage (kWh/day), influence score, new planned spots.

Process

  • Assessment of current state
  • Basic analysis and site selection
  • Coordination of next steps
  • Refinement of data foundation
  • Delivery of comprehensive analysis
  • Support in project organization and implementation
