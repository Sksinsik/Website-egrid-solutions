Problem

End Consumer

Charging Point Operator

Unreliable foundation in location planning, lack of databases and actionable insights, suboptimal location choices, overlooking high-potential sites.

Our Approach

Data-based location assessment

Connecting financial and spatial analysis with key factors for high-traffic locations.

Evaluation and optimization

Determining high-potential sites and optimization of active charging points using data.

Identification of key factors

Essential factors for high-traffic locations are identified to ensure sustainable infrastructure growth.

Comprehensive data evaluation

We combine multiple datasets to refine site selection and improve location planning accuracy.

Data Basis

Charging stations with known energy consumption, POIs around charging stations, weighting by type and distance, regression model for predicted energy usage (kWh/day), influence score, and evaluation of new planned sites.
